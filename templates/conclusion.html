Afterthoughts...

  • Based on the data shown, we cannot say for certain if neighborhood safety directly affects real estate prices. Since we have a vast amount of data, crime rates and types vary between neighborhoods.
  • This application was created specifically for Baltimore real estate consumers and their desire for more knowledge about their neighborhood safety in relation to what they would be paying. The application serves to give the user more capability to "dig deeper" into the details of crime type and neighborhood.
