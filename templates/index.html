Baltimore Real Estate & Crime Visualization App (BRECVA) Usage:

This application is intended for Baltimore, MD consumers who may be interested in the safety of the neighborhood they are looking to purchase or move into. This is a complete set of victim based crime data layered with real estate pricing.

  1. Zoom in to desired neighborhood of interest to visualize what type of crime exists; you are also welcome to deselect any crime type to ignore.
  2. For a closer visualization, click on the neighborhood for the stacked chart below the map.
  3. Each stacked chart includes all crime data associated with the selected neighborhood broken down by totals.
Source: https://www.kaggle.com/sohier/crime-in-baltimore (2012-2017, City of Baltimore)
